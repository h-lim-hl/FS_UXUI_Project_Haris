Appetizers

  • Bruschetta:
    Slices of toasted bread topped with a mixture of diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, and sometimes mozzarella cheese.
  • Caprese Salad:
    A classic Italian salad made with fresh mozzarella cheese, tomatoes, and basil, drizzled with olive oil and balsamic glaze.
  • Stuffed Mushrooms:
    Mushroom caps filled with a savory mixture of breadcrumbs, cheese (such as Parmesan), garlic, herbs, and sometimes sausage or spinach.
  • Antipasto Platter:
    A selection of cured meats (such as prosciutto and salami), cheeses (such as mozzarella and provolone), olives, marinated vegetables (like artichokes and roasted red peppers), and crusty bread or breadsticks.
