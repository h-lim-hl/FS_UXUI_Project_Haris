Drinks

  • Espresso:
    A concentrated coffee beverage brewed by forcing a small amount of nearly boiling water through finely-ground coffee beans. It forms the base for many other coffee drinks.
  • Cappuccino:
    An espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Italy, consisting of espresso and steamed milk, with a layer of frothy milk foam on top.
  • Latte:
    A coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. It has a higher proportion of milk to espresso compared to a cappuccino and is typically topped with a small amount of milk foam.
  • Chai Latte:
    A spiced tea beverage made with black tea, milk, a blend of spices (such as cinnamon, cardamom, ginger, and cloves), and sweetened with sugar or honey. It's often served hot but can also be enjoyed cold.
