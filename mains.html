Mains

  • Spaghetti Bolognese:
    A classic Italian dish featuring spaghetti pasta topped with a rich tomato-based meat sauce.
  • Fettuccine Alfredo:
    Fettuccine pasta coated in a creamy Alfredo sauce made with butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese.
  • Penne alla Vodka:
    Penne pasta served with a creamy tomato sauce flavored with vodka and typically garnished with basil or parsley.
  • Lasagna:
    Layered pasta sheets with alternating layers of meat (such as ground beef or Italian sausage), tomato sauce, and cheese (mozzarella, ricotta, and Parmesan).
